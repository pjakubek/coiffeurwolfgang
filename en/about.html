Salon Coiffeur Wolfgang

The story

The Salon Coiffeur Wolfgang was founded in 1974 by Wolfgang and Gertraude Schwenzner and has been run since then as a family business. In the year 2000, the salon was taken over by Wolfgang's son, Wolfgang Schwenzner, and has been successfully carried on since then. In 2014, we celebrated our 40th birthday with our long-standing customers and employees!

Our values

In the Salon Coiffeur Wolfgang, great attention is paid to friendly and competent advice as well as a relaxed and family atmosphere. We offer our customers a professional craftsmanship and an ambience to feel good.

Our products

With hair care products and styling products from the Wella and Goldwell companies, as well as through continuous training of our employees, we are always up to date with the latest trends.
